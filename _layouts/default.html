This is UBC M.I.N.T.

Medical Innovation In NeuroTechnology

Mission

To build our capacity to tackle neurotech challenges, foster a collaborative network of professionals, enthusiasts and the general public.

Vision

We aim to bridge clinical and social aspects of neuroscience by accessibly reproducing and innovating upon notable experiments and technologies.

About Us

We are a project under UBC’s BEST (Biomedical Engineering Student Team), populated by a diverse group of students (Nursing, Psych, COGS, Elec Eng, Mech Eng, CompSci, etc) eager to make an impact.
